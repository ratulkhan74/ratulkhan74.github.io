Research

Title: A Study on Non-Invasive Haemoglobin Measurement Techniques.

Technology: Machine Learning, Image Processing, Python, IDE: Spyder

Details: This research is based on a non-invasive way to measure the different component of blood like SpO2, glucose, and hemoglobin. We took about 10seconds video for each subject from body organs like an index finger by different Led-Board. Then we apply different machine learning techniques to predict the blood components.
